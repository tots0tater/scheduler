Steps to scheduling an event:
1. Launch the program
2. Login to your google account and give permissions

Mandatory steps (2-6):
3. Enter an event name
4. Toggle available days
5. Select a start time from the calendar on the right
6. Select an end date from the calendar on the right
7. Enter hours needed

Optional steps (7-8):
8. Enter a location
9. Enter a description for the event

10. Click on the "Create Event" button
11. Navigate to your Google Calendar and see scheduled event!
